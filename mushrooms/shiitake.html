Shiitake

Lentinula edodes

Appearance

Shiitake mushrooms have warm brown caps, lightly textured surfaces, and pale undersides. They grow in firm, umbrella-shaped clusters and are known for their classic gourmet appearance.

Texture and Flavor

Shiitake have a dense, meaty texture and deep umami flavor. When cooked, they become tender with a rich, savory aroma that works well in broths, stir-fries, and roasted dishes.

Culinary Uses

Shiitake pair well with rice dishes, noodles, soups, and sautéed vegetables. Their stems also contribute strong flavor when dried and used in stocks.

Additional Info

Store dried mushroom in a cool, dry place. Rehydrate mushrooms by soaking in warm water for 20–30 minutes until soft. Drain and squeeze lightly before cooking.
