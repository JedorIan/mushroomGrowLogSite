Shiitake

Lentinula edodes

Appearance

Rich, brown caps with a firm, umbrella-like shape
Closely packed gills with a light cream color

Texture and Flavor

Firm and meaty texture
Deep and savory umami flavor

Culinary Uses

Great for:
Soups and broths
Ramen
Stir-fries

Additional Info

If Dry: Store in a cool, dry place and use within 12 months
To Rehydrate: Soak in warm water for at least 15 minutes
