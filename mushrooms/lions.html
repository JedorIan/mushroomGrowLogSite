Lion's Mane

Hericium erinaceus

Appearance

Striking, white color
Cascading spines resembling a pom pom or a lion's mane
Spines get longer with age making it look shaggier

Texture and Flavor

Firm, meaty interior that naturally shreds
When cooked, it develops a tender and chewy feel resembling crab or lobster
Flavor is mild and savory with a hint of sweetness

Culinary Uses

Great for:
Stir-fries
Pan-seared "steaks"
Vegan/Vegetarian seafood substitute

Additional Info

If Dry: Store in a cool, dry place
To Rehydrate: Soak in warm water for at least 15 minutes
